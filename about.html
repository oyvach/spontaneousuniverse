Research statement

I wish to build qualitative and quantitative understanding of the physics of candidate models of gravity and dark energy in analytically inaccessible regimes by using state of the art simulations. Doing so is a promising avenue to discriminating novel theories, and to prepare their modelling for confrontation with upcoming surveys.
